David Bowie - The iconic singer songwriter.

Here's a time line of David Bowie's life:

  • 1947 - Born in Brixton, London.
  • 1967 - Releases self-titled album.
  • 1969 - Space Oddity is released as a single.
  • 1970 - Releases The Man Who Sold The World, became first widely acclaimed album.
  • 1972 - Releases landmark record The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars, to become widely successful.
  • 1974 - Releases concept album Diamond Dogs.
  • 1980 - Stars in Broadway production of, The Elephant Man.
  • 1981 - Collaboration with Queen for the single Under Pressure.
  • 1984 - Receives first Grammy.
  • 1986 - Stars in cult classic Labyrinth.
  • 1996 - Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
  • 1997 - Receives a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
  • 2006 - Honored with the Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award.
  • 2013 - Releases first album in a decade, The Next Day.
  • 2016 - Releases final album Blackstar.
  • 2016 - dies at the age of 67.
